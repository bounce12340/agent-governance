# Constitution / 憲法層

## English

These are global rules that apply to every law, case, and judgment.

### Non-negotiable rules

- No task may leak secrets or personal data.
- No task may bypass required review stages.
- No output may claim success without evidence.
- No red line may be overridden by a lower-level law.
- No role may judge its own original law without isolation.

### Structural rules

- Legislative defines the law.
- Executive builds within the law.
- Judiciary judges the output.
- Harness Engineering must exist before judgment.
- Rework must have a maximum retry count.

### Mandatory feedback channels

- Judiciary may request a law amendment.
- Executive may request clarification on ambiguous law.
- Both requests must be recorded as formal artifacts.

## 繁體中文

這些是套用在所有法律、案件與判決上的全域規則。

### 不可妥協原則

- 任何任務都不能外洩 secrets 或個人資料。
- 任何任務都不能跳過必要審查階段。
- 任何輸出都不能沒有證據就宣稱成功。
- 任何下位法律都不能覆蓋上位紅線。
- 任何角色都不能在沒有隔離的情況下自己立法自己審。

### 結構規則

- 立法權定義法律。
- 行政權在法律內執行。
- 司法權負責判決。
- Harness Engineering 必須先存在，才能進司法審查。
- Rework 必須有最大重試次數。

### 必要回饋通道

- 司法權可以提出法律修正請求。
- 行政權可以對模糊法律提出澄清請求。
- 這些請求都必須變成正式 artefact。
